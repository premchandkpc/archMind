## What this chunk does

Measures retrieval quality: are the right documents being found?
Without evaluation, you're flying blind.

## Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Recall@5 | % of relevant docs in top 5 | >0.8 |
| Recall@10 | % of relevant docs in top 10 | >0.9 |
| Precision@5 | % of top 5 that are relevant | >0.6 |
| MRR | Mean Reciprocal Rank of first relevant result | >0.7 |
| NDCG@10 | Normalized Discounted Cumulative Gain | >0.7 |
